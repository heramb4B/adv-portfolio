Services High-Impact Services

"Committed to Excellence in Legal Services."

At Adv. Tejvanti Kapale and Associates, we are driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence in every aspect of our legal practice. With a strong foundation of expertise, integrity, and dedication, we deliver personalized and effective solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our commitment to excellence ensures that you receive the highest quality of legal representation, empowering you to navigate legal challenges with confidence and peace of mind. Trust us to be your advocates for justice, integrity, and the pursuit of favorable outcomes.

Marriage Issues

Navigating marriage issues can be emotionally challenging. We offer compassionate guidance and legal support to couples facing marital conflicts, helping them explore options such as mediation, counseling, or legal separation to resolve disputes amicably and protect their interests.

Domestic Violence

Domestic violence can have devastating effects on individuals and families. We provide legal assistance and advocacy for victims of domestic violence, helping them obtain protection orders, seek justice through criminal proceedings, and access support services to ensure their safety and well-being.

Maintenance & Child Custody

In matters of maintenance and child custody, we advocate for the rights of our clients with a focus on the best interests of the child. Our goal is to secure fair and equitable arrangements for financial support and custody, ensuring the welfare and stability of the child in post-divorce or separation scenarios.

Family Counseling

Family counseling plays a crucial role in resolving conflicts and strengthening relationships. We offer professional guidance and support to families in crisis, facilitating open communication, conflict resolution, and reconciliation efforts to foster healthier and more harmonious family dynamics.

All Types of Criminal Cases

With extensive experience in criminal law, we provide robust defense representation for clients facing all types of criminal charges. From minor misdemeanors to serious felonies, we zealously advocate for our clients' rights, ensuring a fair trial and working towards the best possible outcome.

All Types of Labour, Industrial, & Income Tax Court Matters

We offer comprehensive legal services for labor disputes, industrial matters, and income tax court cases. Whether you're an employer or an employee, we provide expert guidance and representation to resolve disputes, protect rights, and ensure compliance with labor and tax laws.

All Types of Civil Cases Matter & Guidance

In civil cases, we offer strategic legal guidance and representation to resolve disputes related to property, contracts, torts, and other civil matters. Our proactive approach and in-depth legal knowledge enable us to navigate complex civil litigation effectively and achieve favorable outcomes for our clients.

All Types of Agreements & Their Registrations

Drafting and registering agreements require meticulous attention to detail and legal expertise. We assist clients in drafting various types of agreements, including contracts, leases, and partnership agreements, ensuring clarity, enforceability, and compliance with legal requirements.

Legal Notices

Legal notices are essential for communicating legal intentions and demands effectively. We provide assistance in drafting and serving legal notices, ensuring compliance with procedural requirements and safeguarding our clients' interests in legal disputes and negotiations.
