About Adv. Tejavanti Kapale

Protecting Your Future with Expert Legal Advice

Specializing in criminal law, I offer comprehensive legal representation from arrest to appeals. With strategic case evaluation and negotiation skills, I aim to minimize the impact of charges through plea bargaining or vigorous trial advocacy. Additionally, I assist clients in post-conviction relief efforts, including appeals and expungement proceedings, to mitigate long-term consequences.

How I Got Here

My journey into the legal profession is a story of dedication, perseverance, and a deep-seated passion for justice. I am Ms. Adv. Tejvanti M. Kapale, the proud founder of Adv. Tejvanti Kapale and Associates, and a member of the esteemed Pune Bar Association.

Educational Foundation
My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Commerce (B.Com.) from Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), Pune. The insights gained during my commerce studies provided me with a solid understanding of business principles, financial systems, and economic frameworks. This foundation proved invaluable as I transitioned into the field of law, enabling me to approach legal challenges with a well-rounded perspective.

Pursuing Law
Driven by a desire to make a significant impact in the legal arena, I pursued my Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree, also from SPPU, Pune. During this rigorous three-year program, I immersed myself in various branches of law, including criminal, civil, family, and corporate law. The diverse curriculum and hands-on training honed my analytical skills and deepened my understanding of the legal system.

The Inception of Adv. Tejvanti Kapale and Associates
In 2019, fueled by a vision to provide accessible, high-quality legal services, I established Adv. Tejvanti Kapale and Associates. From its inception, the firm has been committed to delivering personalized legal solutions, ensuring that each client receives the attention and expertise they deserve. My team and I specialize in criminal law, family law, and legal drafting, offering comprehensive support tailored to the unique needs of each case.

Professional Growth and Commitment
As a member of the Pune Bar Association, I actively engage with fellow legal professionals, staying abreast of the latest developments in law and continuously refining my skills. This affiliation not only enriches my professional network but also reinforces my commitment to ethical practices and lifelong learning.

Looking Forward
The journey thus far has been both challenging and rewarding. Every case handled, every client assisted, and every courtroom experience has further fueled my dedication to the legal profession. At Adv. Tejvanti Kapale and Associates, we strive to uphold justice, protect rights, and deliver favorable outcomes for our clients.

I am excited to continue this journey, advocating for justice with unwavering commitment and compassion. Thank you for taking the time to learn about my path, and I look forward to the opportunity to assist you with your legal needs.
